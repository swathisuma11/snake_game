Snake Game

Game Play:

  • Boundary Blues: The boundaries are off-limits! If your snake touches the edge of the screen, it will get a bonk on the head and the game will be over.
  • Self-Hugging is Dangerous: Your snake loves itself a bit too much. If it touches its own body, it will get tangled up and the game will end.
  • Snake Growth: Every time your snake eats a piece of red food, it grows longer. The more it eats, the longer it gets, and the harder it is to navigate!
  • Speedy Snake: With each piece of food eaten, your snake gets a bit faster. Be prepared for the speed increase!

Scroll down and enjoy guiding your clumsy snake through its red food-filled adventure!
